Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email, and shipping address. This helps us process your orders and deliver products to you.

2. No Browsing Data Collection

We don't collect any type of browsing data. We have no technology for collecting your browsing experience. Your privacy is preserved.

3. Payment Processing

Your payment process is run by your platform. We just see the name of your payment account. We never store your payment details.

4. No Algorithmic Data Usage

We didn't set up any technology to use your data (like what you order) for algorithm processes. Whatever you see in recommendations is made by winning product basis, not by tracking you.

5. Country Recommendations

If you enable country recommendation, you will see your country's trendy products based on your selection. This is optional and fully under your control.

6. Cookie Policy

We only use cookies for saving your account in your browser. We don't use tracking or non-privacy cookies. Your browsing remains private.

7. Your Data Management

You manage your data by Settings → My Data. You can even delete specific data you want. You have full control over your personal information.
